  ```lean

The checklist says: *"If it asks a yes/no question — 'asked whether', 'is it true that', 'does there exist', 'can one' — use Case 1."* and for solved problems: *"replace `answer(sorry)` with `answer(True)` or `answer(False)` accordingly."*
- [x] The RHS correctly formalizes the question as asked: the set of n where every prime factor of n! + 1 is one of the two primes immediately above n is finite.

**Mathematical review of `AdmitsRainbowC4Coloring`:** The definition correctly models:
- An edge-coloring `c : Sym2 (Fin n) → Fin n` using `n` colors on `n` vertices.
- A 4-cycle `a–b–d–e–a` requiring all 4 vertices distinct (6 pairwise `≠` conditions) and all 4 edges present in `G`.
- The rainbow condition via all 6 = C(4,2) pairwise color inequalities on the 4 edges.

Note: `a ≠ b` and `d ≠ e` are redundant (implied by `G.Adj` on a `SimpleGraph`), but harmless.

### `answer()` usage

The original problem asks "What is the order of growth of $F(N)$?" — literally a "What is" question. However, the answer (asymptotic growth $N^{1/4+o(1)}$) is not a single concrete value but rather an asymptotic statement best expressed as a pair of bounds. The problem is also marked `research solved`, with the answer known to be $N^{1/4+o(1)}$.

This is the most natural and mathematically precise way to express the asymptotic result, since `answer()` is not well-suited for capturing "$o(1)$" exponent behavior.

The current formalization instead states a direct lower-bound inequality (Case 3 style) without using `answer()` at all. Additionally, the formalization only captures the lower bound direction — the docstring notes the bound is best possible but the theorem does not assert tightness (i.e., existence of a configuration achieving equality).

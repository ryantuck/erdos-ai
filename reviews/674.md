  ```lean

      ∃ x y z : ℕ, x > 1 ∧ y > 1 ∧ z > 1 ∧ x ^ x * y ^ y = z ^ z := by
    sorry
  ```
  The current formulation uses a direct assertion (Case 3) without `answer()`, which is incorrect for a yes/no question.

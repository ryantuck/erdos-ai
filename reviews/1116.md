The problem is solved affirmatively (Gol'dberg and Toppila constructed such functions), so the correct form is:
    ```lean

        ∃ f : ℂ → ℂ, Differentiable ℂ f ∧
          ∀ a b : ℂ, a ≠ b →
            ∀ (M : ℝ) (R : ℝ), ∃ r : ℝ, r > R ∧
              M * (rootCount f r b : ℝ) < (rootCount f r a : ℝ) := by
      sorry
    ```

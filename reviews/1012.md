The original problem says "Determine or estimate f(k)", which is a "Determine" phrasing suggesting **Case 2** (`= answer(...)`). However, the formalization takes a different — and arguably stronger — approach: rather than expressing `f(k) = answer(2*k + 3)`, it directly states Woodall's full theorem as a **Case 3** assertion (that every sufficiently large graph with enough edges contains cycles of all lengths 3 ≤ ℓ ≤ n−k). The docstring explicitly notes "this answers Erdős' original question with f(k) = 2k + 3."

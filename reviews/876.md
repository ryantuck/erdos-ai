### Formalization of `IsSumFreeErdos`

The definition states: for all $a \in A$, no nonempty subset $S \subseteq A$ of elements strictly less than $a$ can sum to $a$. `Finset` enforces element distinctness automatically. Since all elements of $S$ are strictly less than $a$, `a ∉ S` is automatically satisfied. This correctly captures "no element of $A$ is the sum of finitely many distinct smaller elements of $A$". **Correct**.

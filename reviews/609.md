### `answer()` usage

The original problem says: *"Let f(n) be the minimal m such that… Estimate f(n)."*

"Estimate" is close to Case 2 ("Find", "Determine"), but the mathematical meaning of "estimate f(n)" is to provide asymptotic bounds, not a single closed-form value. The formalization breaks the problem into three direct assertions (Case 3):

1. **Main theorem**: f(n) is finite (every n-coloring of K_{2^n+1} has a monochromatic odd cycle).
2. **Lower bound variant**: f(n) ≥ 2^{c√(log n)}.
3. **Upper bound variant**: f(n) ≤ C · n^{3/2} · 2^{n/2}.
